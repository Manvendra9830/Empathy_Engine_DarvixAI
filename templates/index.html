Voice Parameters

        

          

            🎵
            
Pitch controls how high or low the voice sounds. Higher pitch signals excitement; lower pitch signals gravity or frustration.

          

          

            ⏱️
            
Rate controls how fast speech is spoken. Fast rate energises; slow rate conveys weight or concern.

          

          

            🔊
            
Volume controls loudness. Louder speech can convey urgency; softer speech conveys empathy.

          

          

            ⏸️
            
Pauses inserted via SSML <break> tags add drama and let emotional weight land.
